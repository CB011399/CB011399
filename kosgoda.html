Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

Explore the beautiful hatchery in kosgoda!

The Hatchery

Kosgoda turtle hatchery is a fantastic place to visit if you're interested in learning about sea turtles and their conservation.The hatchery is run by a team of dedicated and knowledgable staff who are passionate about protecting these amazing creatures.They provide a safe environment for turtle eggs to hatch and for baby turtles to grow before being released into the wild.The hatchery also works to rescue and rehabilitate injured turtles and to raise awareness about the turtles facing sea turtles in the wild.

One of the highlights of a visit to kosgoda turtle hatchery is the opportunity to see the baby turtles up close.Visitors can hold and feed the turtles,and even release them into the sea.It's unique and unforgettable experience that will leave you with a deep appreciation for these amazing creatures.The hatchery also provides a great opportunity to learn about the different species of sea turtles,their lifecycle and the challenges they face in the wild.It's a fun and educational experience that is perfect for all ages.

Best Things To Do In The Hatchery!



Learn about sea turtles: The hatchery provides a great opportunity to learn about the different species of sea turtles,their lifecycle and the challenges they face in the wild



Witness the hatching of turtle eggs: Visitors can witness the hatching of turtle eggs and the release of baby turtles into the sea.



Hold and feed baby turtles:Visitors can hold and feed the baby turtles,and even take a photo with them.



Visit the gift shop: The hatchery has a gift shop where you can purchase souvenirs and support their conservation work.



Support the conservation work: By visiting the hatchery and purchasing souvenirs you are supporting their conservation efforts and helping to protect these amazing creatures..
